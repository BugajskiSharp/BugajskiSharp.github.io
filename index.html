About Me

This is what I do

Krisha Bugajski-Sharp is a data scientist with a strong foundation in both analytical methods and environmental science. She earned her Master of Science in Data Science from Merrimack College, graduating with a 4.0 GPA, after completing her Bachelor of Science in Marine Science with highest honors at the University of Hawai‘i at Hilo. Her academic journey reflects a deep interest in understanding complex systems through data.

Her work centers on transforming complex datasets into meaningful, actionable insights. She has experience building predictive models, exploring patterns through segmentation, and creating clear, compelling visualizations using Python, R, and SQL. Through projects such as predicting marathon finishing times using environmental conditions and modeling customer behavior in booking and retention datasets, she has developed a strong ability to clean data, engineer features, evaluate model performance, and communicate results effectively to both technical and non-technical audiences.

In addition to her technical work, Krisha brings hands-on experience from environmental and field-based roles with organizations such as the USDA Forest Service and AmeriCorps. In these positions, she collected and analyzed ecological and fire behavior data, supported data-informed decision-making in dynamic environments, and collaborated with multidisciplinary teams. This combination of real-world experience and technical expertise allows her to approach problems with both analytical rigor and practical context, making her well-suited for data science and analytics roles across a variety of industries.
